## Important Notes for Writing

1. **Verify CEI status before publishing** — The Canadian Entrepreneurs' Incentive was proposed in 2024 Budget, may have been modified. Confirm whether it's active, cancelled, or deferred as of publish date.
2. **Inclusion rate** — The 2/3 inclusion rate increase was cancelled (March 2025). Stays at 50%. Verify this hasn't changed by publish date.
3. **This is NOT tax advice** — Include a disclaimer. "This is general information for planning purposes. Consult a tax professional for advice specific to your situation."
4. **Target audience** — Canadian small business owners ($500K-$50M revenue) considering selling in the next 1-5 years. Write for the founder, not the accountant.
5. **Tone** — Plain language, direct, practical. Avoid tax jargon where possible. When you must use a technical term, define it immediately.
